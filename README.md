### Computer Engineering (Hons, Distinction) · University of Queensland · Brisbane

Firmware that runs on real silicon and AI systems that ship to real clients. The through-line is the same: understand the hardware or the problem deeply, then build something reliable on top of it.

Spent the last year tutoring Advanced Embedded Systems at UQ and co-founding a small AI startup. Two very different things, but both taught the same lesson: the gap between "works on my desk" and "works in production" is where the interesting problems live.

---

## What I build

**Firmware and Embedded Systems**

Real-time systems on STM32, ESP32 and nRF52840. FreeRTOS and Zephyr RTOS with multi-task architectures, ISR design and task synchronisation. Register-level SPI, I2C and UART peripheral drivers written without HAL abstractions. BLE GATT stacks, NRF24L01+ radio protocols with Hamming error correction, Kalman filtering and sensor fusion across multiple microcontrollers.

**AI Engineering**

Production RAG pipelines, multi-agent orchestration and LLM integrations deployed for real clients via [SingSinghAI](https://www.singsinghai.com.au/). A tax automation platform that cut a 4-hour manual process to 30 minutes. An AI calling assistant handling 20 calls a day with 70% resolved without human handoff. End-to-end on AWS with Docker, Terraform, GitHub Actions and Grafana.
